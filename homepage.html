What's home cooking?

Home cooking means preparing meals with fresh raw ingredients instead of using pre-packaged or processed foods.

Why Choose home cooking?

After a day of hard work, many people choose to eat out or ordering since they are the quickest, easiest choice. However, convenience and processed food can harm our health. Convenience food always has a lot of chemical additives, hormones, sugar, salt, fat and calories, which are bad for our body. Besides, when cooking at home, you can have more control over the ingredients, which lets you make sure you can enjoy fresh and wholesome meals. Besides, cooking at home can help you to save money.

How to home cooking?

One important thing about cooking at home is choosing the right ingredients for yourself. When buying ingredients choose fresh ones that you like and make sure that you will definitely eat them. After you have chosen the ingredients you need, you can start by prepping the ingredients. Cut and portion meats according to your serving size so that you can take them out and use them every time you need them. Use different preservation methods for different vegetables and fruits to maximize the best taste. For example, you can cut onions up and place them in Tupperware, taking them out when needed. Prepping the ingredients first can greatly reduce the number of tedious steps during cooking. Finally, try different recipes as you can, and when you find your favourite ones, keep a record of the recipe so that you can cook it again in the future.
