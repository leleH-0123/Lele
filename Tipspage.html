Some Tips for healthy eating habits

Try to enjoy your food

It's important for you to choose several kinds of food to eat. Choose the foods that you enjoy based on your cultures and food traditions. Familiar things can always make us feel comfortable. Cook the food in the way you like and try to enjot them can help you better living a healthy eating habit.

limit highly processed foods

When eating at home it is more easily for you to make healthy choices. Try to decrease the amount of highly processed food you buy and eat. Decrease the amount of sugars, sodium or saturated fat in your meals, instead, you can add more vegetables, fruit, whole grain foods and plant-based protein foods to your meals.

16+8

The 16:8 plan is a form of time-restricted fasting. It means eating foods during an 8-hour period and avoiding food or fasting for the remaining 16 hours each day. It can help you get weight loss and fat loss. When in the 16:8 plan, it's important to focus on eating nutritious foods, it can make the process more beneficial.
