Fried Salmon

Ingredients

2*150g salmon fillets without skin

olive oil

unsalted butter

some lemon (optional)

Steps

  1. Season salmon fillets with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Heat the oil and butter in a nonstick skillet over medium heat, swirling the pan until melted and bubbly.
  3. Then turn the heat up. When the butter begins to bubble, add the salmon fillets to the pan, skin side down, and saute for 3 minutes or until crisp.
  4. Turn the fillets over and cook over low heat for another 2 minutes, then drizzle with lemon juice.
  5. Transfer the salmon to a serving dish and baste with the remaining butter sauce in the pan.
