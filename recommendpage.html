Recommend Foods

Broccoli


Tomato


Bell pepper


Beef

Benefits

Broccoli has low calories and can provide you with the full daily requirement of Vitamin K and twice the daily recommended amount of Vitamin C. It can also prevent cancer in several organs, like the bladder, liver, breasts and stomach.

Tomatoes can provide you with 427 mg of potassium and 24.7 mg of Vitamin C. Tomatoes contain lycopene, which is a strong antioxidant that can help prevent prostate cancer.


Sweet bell peppers can be red, green, orange or yellow. Bell pepers combine several kinds of nutrition. A cup of chopped red bell pepper can combine 38.7 calories, 190 mg of Vitamin C, 0.434 mg of Vitamin B6, folate and Vitamin A.


Beef can provide people with several essential nutrients including protein, iron, zinc, Vitamin B6, Vitamin B12 and potassium. These nutrients are important to our body and difficult to get from plant-based foods. Beef is more nutrient-dense than many other protein foods.
