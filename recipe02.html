Roast Chicken

Ingredients

1 onion, rougly chopped

vegetables like potato, broccoli

1 chicken

an apple

butter

olive oil

soy sauce

honey

some lemon (optional)

Steps

  1. Soak the chicken in salt water for 2 hours to clean the blood.
  2. Mix 1 tablespoon of salt,1 tablespoon of black pepper,2 tablespoons of soy sauce, and 2 tablespoons of honey, apply them to the chicken, cover it with plastic wrap, and marinate all night.
  3. Cut the apple and onion into pieces and stuff them into the chicken's stomach with toothpicks. After fixing them, tie them with a rope tied to the wings and legs. Use oven preheated at 130 degrees and bake for 90min, every half hour brush the chicken with butter honey sauce.
  4. Wash the vegetables and cut them into pieces, toss them with salt, chilli powder and olive oil.
  5. After 90 minutes, put the vegetables in, brush the chicken again with butter and honey sauce, and continue to bake for 30-40min.
